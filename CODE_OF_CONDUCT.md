# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically constructive community. Participation must make it easier for people to learn, contribute, review, and disagree safely.

Expected behavior includes:

- treating people with respect regardless of experience or background;
- giving specific feedback about work rather than attacking a person;
- acknowledging mistakes and correcting them openly;
- respecting privacy, consent, attribution, and intellectual property;
- keeping project spaces focused on useful collaboration.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, unwanted sexual attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, sustained disruption, or publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, community spaces, and public interactions when someone is representing this project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject contributions, restrict participation, or ban a participant when behavior violates this policy. Enforcement should be proportionate, documented privately, and focused on safety and restoration where possible.

## Reporting

Do not report conduct incidents in a public issue. Contact the repository owner through a private contact method listed on the owner's [GitHub profile](https://github.com/xuanbui79zombie-svg). Include links, dates, context, and any evidence you are comfortable sharing.

Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a reporter or witness is prohibited. If an immediate safety risk exists, contact the appropriate local service first.
