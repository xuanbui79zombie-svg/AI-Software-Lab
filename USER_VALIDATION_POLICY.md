# Real-User Validation Policy / 真实用户验证政策

## Policy / 政策

Real-user validation is **opt-in and off by default** for every portfolio project. It may be planned or executed only when the project owner explicitly requests it for that specific project.

所有 Portfolio 项目的真实用户验证均为**选择加入，默认关闭**。只有项目负责人针对具体项目明确提出要求时，才能规划或执行。

## Default Behavior / 默认行为

- Do not create recruitment, interview, usability-session, incentive, outreach, or participant-data tasks by default.
- Do not contact people, collect consent, or process participant information without an explicit project-specific request.
- Record the status as `Not requested` or `Skipped by owner policy`; never record it as passed or completed.
- Retain reusable research documents only as inactive optional templates. Do not instantiate or schedule them automatically.
- Do not penalize a project, block a release, or lower its portfolio readiness solely because real-user validation was not requested.

- 默认不创建招募、访谈、可用性会话、激励、外联或参与者数据任务。
- 没有针对具体项目的明确要求时，不联系任何人、不收集同意，也不处理参与者信息。
- 状态必须记录为 `Not requested` 或 `Skipped by owner policy`，不得写成已通过或已完成。
- 调研文档只能作为未启用的可选模板保留，不得自动实例化或排期。
- 不得仅因未要求真实用户验证而阻止发布、降低 Portfolio 就绪度或处罚项目。

## What Remains Required / 仍然必须执行的验证

This policy does not weaken engineering quality gates. Automated tests, code review, security, privacy, accessibility, compatibility, performance, deployment smoke tests, and documentation checks remain required according to project risk.

本政策不降低工程质量门禁。自动化测试、代码评审、安全、隐私、可访问性、兼容性、性能、部署冒烟测试和文档检查仍须按项目风险执行。

## Evidence Boundary / 证据边界

When real-user validation is not requested, project documentation must not claim user validation, adoption, usability, satisfaction, market demand, product-market fit, or commercial outcomes. Product assumptions may remain documented as assumptions or unknowns.

未要求真实用户验证时，项目文档不得声称已获得用户验证、采用率、可用性、满意度、市场需求、产品市场匹配或商业结果。相关内容只能标记为假设或未知项。

## Opt-In Procedure / 启用流程

The owner can opt in at any time by explicitly requesting real-user validation and defining the project, decision to inform, scope, participant and consent rules, privacy boundaries, and required evidence. Only then may the team create research tasks and acceptance gates.

负责人可随时明确要求启用，但必须指定项目、需要支持的决策、范围、参与者与同意规则、隐私边界和所需证据。完成这些定义后，团队才可创建调研任务和验收门禁。
